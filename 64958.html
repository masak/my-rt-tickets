This (untested) version will use the 'log10' method regardless of PMC
type, so it doesn't haven't to be re-extended for another numeric PMC
later.

.sub 'log10' :multi(num)
.param num z
$N0 = log10 z
.return($N0)
.end

.sub 'log10' :multi(_)
.param pmc z
$P0 = z.'log10'()
.return($P0)
.end
